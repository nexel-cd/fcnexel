Why Study in IRELAND ?

Ireland offers a unique blend of tradition, religion, and history that shapes its culture and self-perception, making it a distinctive study abroad destination for Indian students. With a high number of universities ranked in the top 500 globally and research-focused institutions in the top 1% in 19 fields, students can benefit from world-class education and join innovative research programs.


Additionally, studying in Ireland provides a vibrant city life and access to strategic research facilities of some of the world's biggest and best companies. Overall, studying in Ireland can be a life-changing and worthwhile decision for Indian students.

Career Opportunities in IRELAND

Ireland offers international students studying full-time courses the opportunity to work up to 20 hours per week during term-time and full-time during semester breaks. Additionally, completing a degree from a recognized institution allows students to stay back in Ireland for up to 12 months and 6 months for an Ordinary Bachelor's Degree to search for employment and apply for a Green Card/Work Permit.


Ireland has a strong economy with industries in Pharmaceuticals, International Services, Manufacturing, and Software Development. It is home to 32 FDA-approved Manufacturing plants producing 6 out of the 10 World's best-selling drugs. Google's European Headquarters is located in Dublin, reflecting the country's reputation for excellence in education and modern economy.

Education Cost in IRELAND

The tuition fees for studying abroad in Ireland vary depending on the institution and the course of study. For example, undergraduate and postgraduate non-EU students in medicine and related fields can expect to pay between €12,035 and €51,635, while those in engineering, science and technology, business, and arts and humanities can expect to pay between €10,000 and €30,000.


In addition to tuition fees, the cost of living in Ireland for Indian students is estimated to be between €8,000 and €12,000 annually, depending on location and lifestyle.

IRELAND Student VISA

International students who wish to study in Ireland must apply for a valid visa, which usually takes 4 to 5 weeks to process. It is essential to apply well in advance of the start of the course. To obtain a visa, students must provide mandatory documents, including a Letter of Acceptance from the college confirming their enrollment in a full-time course of at least 15 hours of organized daytime study per week, along with evidence of financial support for tuition fees and living expenses.


International students pursuing higher studies must also provide academic transcripts, personal information, and evidence of financial strength equaling €10,000 annually for living expenses. Visa counselors at Edwise, a study in Ireland consultant, are professionally trained to assist students with visa formalities. Student visas in Ireland are typically issued for the entire course duration and are Multi-Entry Visas.
