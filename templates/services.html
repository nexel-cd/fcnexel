Our Services

Career Counselling

At Future Counselors, we understand that choosing the right career path can be overwhelming, but our expert career advisors are here to make the process easy and enjoyable for you.

We carefully analyze your student profile and provide personalized guidance to help you achieve your dream career. Our team of highly qualified advisors will help you navigate questions like "Which country is best for my studies?" and "Which course will set me on the path to success?" We'll also advise you on which universities are recognized and have a high placement record.

Let us be your trusted guide as you make important decisions about your future.

Course, program, or university selection Advice

At our consulting firm, we know that studying abroad can be a daunting experience. That's why we're here to guide you through the process and ensure you make the best decision for your academic interests and requirements.

We understand that choosing the right course and university is crucial to your success, which is why we work tirelessly to find you the best options from around the world. Our goal is to make sure you're not just satisfied, but thrilled with your decision. Let us help you make your dreams a reality.

Admission Application Processing

Admission to top institutions can be a challenging process, but with Future Counselors by your side, you'll be equipped with the tools and expertise you need to stand out from the crowd. Our expert guidance will help you navigate the complex application process and understand the expectations of top universities.

We'll assist you in crafting a winning Statement of Purpose (SOP) that highlights your strengths and sets you apart from other applicants. Plus, we'll make sure all your documentation is in order, giving you the best chance of success. Trust us to help you make a strong impression and secure your spot at the institution of your dreams.

Financial Aid & Scholarship

At Future Counselors, we understand that financing your education abroad can be a daunting task. That's why our expert counselors stay up-to-date on the latest scholarship opportunities and can guide you on managing your finances. Whether you need assistance with personal finances or a bank loan, we'll help you find the most cost-effective and convenient way to fund your education. We have established partnerships with top financial institutions to ensure you get the best possible assistance.

We're proud to be one of the few educational consultants in India offering this valuable service to our students. Our past students have secured scholarships from top universities and even received national overseas scholarship schemes from the government of India. Let us help you achieve your academic dreams without the financial burden.

Applications

Applying for a traveling application is not always a straightforward process. Each individual is different and the standard rules on government advices changes accordingly. Future Counselors Education advisors are well versed with the application requirements of different countries and provide assistance regarding application. We break down the complex legislation surrounding the way that is easy for you to understand and act upon. Our fully trained and experience counselors take care of the bureaucracy for you and prepare you for interviews, if needed.

Pre Departure Orientation

Pre-departure orientations are conducted to enable you to have a smooth departure to the destination and a comfortable assimilation on landing. Our trained and experienced counselors run a number of pre-departure briefing sessions with the aim of orientating you to your study destination.

Travel & Forex Assistance

Future Counselors helps students to find the best possible way to reach the study destination at ease. We assist them with flight bookings, Insurance needs (if any). We also facilitate foreign exchange and remittance of fees etc.

Post Departure Assistance

Future Counselors offers post arrival assistance to the students in various universities across the world. Future Counselors assists airport pickups, temporary accommodation for the students in collaboration with respective universities.
